HTML <h1> to <h6> Tag Example

HTML Code:

<h1>This is heading 1</h1>
<p>Paragraph under heading 1</p>

<h2>This is heading 2</h2>
<p>Paragraph under heading 2</p>

<h3>This is heading 3</h3>
<p>Paragraph under heading 3</p>

<h4>This is heading 4</h4>
<p>Paragraph under heading 4</p>

<h5>This is heading 5</h5>
<p>Paragraph under heading 5</p>

<h6>This is heading 6</h6>
<p>Paragraph under heading 6</p>

Output in Browser:

This is heading 1

Paragraph under heading 1

This is heading 2

Paragraph under heading 2

This is heading 3

Paragraph under heading 3

This is heading 4

Paragraph under heading 4

This is heading 5

Paragraph under heading 5

This is heading 6

Paragraph under heading 6

Note: The <h1> to <h6> tags define headings from largest to smallest. Use them to organize your webpage content into sections. This improves both readability and SEO (search engine optimization).
